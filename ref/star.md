Create a star shape.

    g.star({x: 0, y: 0}, 5, 100, 20);

## Parameters
- `position`: The center point of the star.
- `points`: The amount of points in the star.
- `outer`: The outer diameter of the star.
- `inner`: The inner diameter of the star.
